About cactus

Cactus Restaurant has asserted itself as the go-to restaurant for mouth-watering delicacies in Victoria Island, Lagos. A meal at Cactus will leave you asking for more. The restaurant offers a great view of the Lagos Lagoon from its terrace, making it a great place for a romantic date, family outing or a group hang-out. The Cactus menu offers a wide variety of meals including pizza, seafood and chicken dishes. - hotels.ng
